\section{Capture --- the Penning-Trap Three-Frequency Invariance Keystone}
\label{app:penning}

This appendix is the full data record for pipeline process
\textcircled{\scriptsize 3}, the funnel's keystone. It will state the three
ideal-Penning eigenfrequencies (axial $\omega_z$, modified-cyclotron
$\omega_+$, magnetron $\omega_-$), the trapping condition
$\omega_c\ge\sqrt2\,\omega_z$, and the Brown--Gabrielse invariance theorem
$\omega_c^2=\omega_+^2+\omega_z^2+\omega_-^2$ with its sum
($\omega_++\omega_-=\omega_c$) and product
($\omega_+\omega_-=\tfrac12\omega_z^2$) corollaries
\citep{brown1986geonium,dehmelt1990}. Every digit traces to the
representative antiproton anchor ($B=5$\,T, $U_0=10$\,V, $d=5$\,mm) and the
\tierGreen\ atoms \texttt{penning\_omega\_plus} / \texttt{penning\_omega\_minus}
/ \texttt{penning\_invariance} (residual $|\Delta|=0.0$) in
\texttt{companion/verify-ledger.json}.
