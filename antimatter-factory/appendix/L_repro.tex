\section{Reproducibility Runbook}
\label{app:repro}

This appendix is the step-by-step reproduction runbook. It will give the
exact commands to rebuild the verify binary
(\texttt{tool/build\_hexa\_verify.sh} $\to$ \texttt{bin/hexa-verify}, run on
host \texttt{mini} with \texttt{POOL\_DISABLE=1} per the verify-host
constraint), enumerate the 22 hexa-native functions and their canonical
stdlib home, list the inlined CODATA-2018 constants
\citep{mohr2018codata} ($m_p$, $e$, $\mu_B$, $k_B$, $R_\infty$; CPT
$m_{\bar p}=m_p$), point to the inherited magnet decks under
\texttt{stdlib/rtsc/} and \texttt{stdlib/material/magnet/}, and give the
paper-build (\texttt{make}), figure (\texttt{make figures}), and lint
(\texttt{make lint}) targets plus the \texttt{companion/} rebuild steps.
